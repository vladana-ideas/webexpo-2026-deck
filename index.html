case 2 · the moves

what we
fixed.

teach the value. across every touchpoint.
  • solutions page — rewritten for value
  • resources page — supporting materials
  • comparison pages — honest where we win, where we don't, why we cost less
  • 7-email onboarding series one feature per day
  • bi-weekly product reminders releases, new features
  • in-app onboarding push advanced features in trial

case 3 · diagnosis

what was
wrong.

retention bleeds where pmf is thin.
  • icp too broad — "for everyone" = for nobody
  • stripe migration → weak product-market fit
  • trial only 7 days — not enough time to value
  • no community, no shared space
  • no safety net — users left in silence

rolling out.

  • trial 7 → 14 days

  • community channel — discord, slack, app, events
  • product validation reset user interviews, power users shape the roadmap
  • monthly q&a calls
  • churn safety net discounts, exit interviews, win-back follow-up
